What we do

Modern chip design is becoming increasingly complex, with verification bottlenecks slowing iteration and increasing the risk of errors. heraion addresses these challenges by integrating AI-powered models directly into existing Cadence and Synopsys workflows, enabling designers to automate verification tasks without changing their current processes.

By leveraging advanced AI, heraion not only accelerates verification but also helps identify potential design issues early, reduces costly errors, and improves overall performance. Designers can iterate faster, optimize their designs more effectively, and focus on innovation rather than repetitive checks.

In an era where speed and accuracy are critical, heraion transforms the chip design workflow into a smarter, more efficient process, seamlessly bridging traditional design tools with next-generation AI capabilities.
